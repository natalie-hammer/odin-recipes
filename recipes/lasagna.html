Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for lasagna, a layered pasta and meat sauce dish.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl.
  2. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add diced onions.
  3. Add the ground beef and sausage and increase heat to medium-high.
  4. Lay cooled noodles flat on wax or parchment paper while assembling the lasagna.
