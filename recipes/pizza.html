Pizza Recipe

Description

A recipe for making pizza, a flat pie with savory toppings.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Red sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Combine the flour, yeast and water in a bowl.
  2. Knead the dough until it is soft and pliable.
  3. Cover the dough in a large bowl with a damp towel. Let sit for a few hours.
  4. Roll out the dough into large circles.
  5. Apply sauce, cheese and any other toppings.
  6. Bake in the oven at 375 for 20 minutes.
